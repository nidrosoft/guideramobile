# Guidera — User Testing Script

> **Platform:** UserTesting.com (or equivalent remote testing platform)
> **App Type:** Expo React Native (iOS/Android via TestFlight or Expo Go)
> **Target Testers:** 10-20 participants
> **Duration:** 45-60 minutes per session
> **Recording:** Audio recording required (transcript will be extracted)

---

   - No → Still qualify

3. **What type of smartphone do you use?**
   - iPhone (iOS 15+) → Qualify

   - Yes → Qualify

## SETUP INSTRUCTIONS FOR TESTERS

Please read these instructions carefully before you begin.

**What is Guidera?**
Guidera is an AI-powered travel companion app that helps you plan trips, track flights, discover destinations, connect with other travelers, and get personalized travel recommendations. You are testing a beta version of the app.

**What you need:**
- Your smartphone (iPhone or Android)
- A stable internet connection
- An audio recording app (Voice Memos on iPhone, or any voice recorder)
- Approximately 45-60 minutes of uninterrupted time

**How to record your feedback:**
- Start your audio recording BEFORE you begin testing
- **Think out loud** — narrate everything you're doing, what you see, what you expect, and what surprises you
- If something confuses you, say so. If something delights you, say that too
- If you encounter a bug or something that doesn't work, describe exactly what happened
- Keep recording until you've completed all tasks

**How to install the app:**
- [Instructions will be provided based on distribution method — TestFlight link for iOS or Expo Go QR code]

**Important:** This is a beta product. Some features use sample data. Your goal is to test the user experience, navigation, and overall flow — not just whether data is "real."

## SECTION 1: ONBOARDING & SIGN UP
**Estimated time: 5-7 minutes**

### Task 1.1 — Download & First Launch
> Open the app for the first time. Describe your first impression of the launch screen and any loading experience.

**What to observe:**
- Does the app load smoothly?
- What do you notice first?
- Does anything feel slow or broken?

### Task 1.2 — Create Your Account
> Sign up for a new account using your email or phone number. Go through the entire sign-up process.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Was the sign-up process straightforward?
- Did you encounter any errors?
- How long did it take?

### Task 1.3 — Complete Onboarding
> Go through the onboarding flow. Select your travel preferences, interests, and any other information the app asks for. Complete every step.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Were the onboarding questions relevant to you as a traveler?
- Did any step feel unnecessary or confusing?
- Did the app explain why it's asking for this information?
- How did you feel after completing onboarding? Excited to use the app? Overwhelmed?

## SECTION 2: PROFILE SETUP
**Estimated time: 5 minutes**

### Task 2.1 — Navigate to Account Settings
> From the home screen, find and open your account/profile settings. Look for a gear icon or your profile picture.

**What to observe:**
- Was it easy to find the settings?
- What options do you see?

### Task 2.2 — Update Your Profile
> Tap on "Edit Profile" and update the following information:
> - First name and last name
> - Add a bio/description (write anything, e.g., "Adventure seeker who loves local food")
> - Add your phone number
> - Set your location
> - Upload or change your profile photo (optional)
> 
> Save your changes.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Were all the fields easy to find and edit?
- Did the save action give you clear confirmation?
- Did you notice any fields that didn't work or seemed broken?

### Task 2.3 — Explore Account Settings
> Browse through the other settings options. Check out:
> - Notification preferences — toggle a few on/off
> - Privacy settings
> - Any other settings that catch your eye
> 
> Don't change anything critical, just explore and share your thoughts.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Are the settings organized in a way that makes sense?
- Is there anything missing that you'd expect to find here?

## SECTION 3: EXPLORE PAGE — SEARCH & DISCOVERY
**Estimated time: 8-10 minutes**

### Task 3.1 — Explore the Home Screen
> Take a moment to scroll through the home/explore page. Look at every section — deals, destinations, categories, and any cards or content.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- What stands out to you on this page?
- Do the deals and destinations look interesting?
- Is the layout easy to understand?

### Task 3.2 — Use the Search Bar
> Tap on the search bar at the top of the explore page. Search for a destination you're interested in (e.g., "Tokyo", "Paris", "Bali"). 

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Did search results appear quickly?
- Are the results relevant to what you searched?
- Can you find useful information about the destination?

### Task 3.3 — Browse Destination Details
> From your search results, tap on a destination to view its detail page. Explore all the information shown — photos, description, tips, weather, events, etc.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Is the destination page informative?
- Would this help you decide whether to visit?
- Is there any information missing that you'd want to see?

### Task 3.4 — Interact with Deals Section
> Go back to the explore page. Look at the deals section ("See Our Deals"). Tap on a deal card to see its details.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Are the deal prices and savings clear?
- Would you trust these deals?
- Is the deal detail page useful?

### Task 3.5 — Browse Category Pills & Sections
> Scroll through the explore page and interact with:
> - The category filter pills (All, Museum, Safari, Mountain, etc.) — tap a few
> - The "Popular Destinations" section — tap "View All" and browse
> - The "Trending" content and TikTok/creator section if visible
> - Any other sections you see

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Do the categories help you find what you're looking for?
- Does the content in each section look relevant and up-to-date?
- Is the TikTok/creator content section useful?

## SECTION 4: TRIP CREATION & IMPORT
**Estimated time: 10-12 minutes**

### Task 4.1 — Import a Trip via Photo Scan
> Navigate to the Trips tab. Look for the option to import a trip. Select the option to import by scanning a boarding pass photo.
> 
> **Before this test:** Generate a fake boarding pass image using ChatGPT, MidJourney, or any AI image tool. Ask it to create "a realistic airline boarding pass for a flight departing in 3-4 hours from [your nearest airport] to any destination." Use this image for scanning.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Was it easy to find the import trip feature?
- Did the photo scan work? What happened after scanning?
- Did the app correctly detect the flight information?
- If a trip was created, does it look accurate?

### Task 4.2 — Explore the Trip Card
> If a trip was created from the scan (or if there's an existing trip), look at the trip card on the Trips tab. Notice:
> - The trip status (upcoming, ongoing)
> - The countdown timer on the home page
> - The trip card details

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Does the trip card give you a good overview?
- Is the status clear?
- Would you find the countdown timer useful?

### Task 4.3 — Try Booking Searches
> Go back to the explore page or use the category icons to try searching for:
> - **Flights** — Search for a flight between two cities
> - **Hotels** — Search for a hotel in a destination
> - **Car Rental** — Search for a car rental
> - **Experiences** — Browse experiences in a destination
> - **Packages** — Try the package builder
> 
> You don't need to complete a booking. Just go through the search and results screens.

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Did each search type work?
- Were the results displayed clearly?
- Is it clear that bookings redirect to external providers?
- Any search that didn't work or felt broken?

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Is the itinerary layout clear and useful?
- Can you easily add activities?
- Does the day-by-day format make sense?

### Task 5.3 — Explore the Packing List Plugin

> - Notice the packing progress bar

**Questions to answer out loud:**

- Was adding items easy?
- Does the progress indicator help?

### Task 5.4 — Explore the Journal Plugin
> Navigate to the Journal section.
> - Try creating a new journal entry
> - Add some text and/or a photo if possible

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Would you use a trip journal?
- Is the entry creation process smooth?

### Task 5.5 — Explore the Expense Tracker Plugin
> Navigate to the Expense Tracker section.
> - Log 5 expenses with different categories (food, transport, shopping, activities, accommodation)

> - Check the budget overview/chart

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Is logging an expense quick and easy?
- Are the categories useful?
- Does the budget visualization help you understand your spending?

### Task 5.6 — Explore Do's & Don'ts Plugin
> Navigate to the Do's & Don'ts section for your destination.
> - Read through the cultural tips
> - Share any feedback on the content

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Is this information useful for a traveler?

### Task 5.7 — Explore the Safety Plugin
> Navigate to the Safety section.
> - Try the currency converter button

**Questions to answer out loud:**
- Does the safety information make you feel more prepared?

## CLOSING QUESTIONS

After completing all tasks, please answer these questions in your audio recording:

1. **Overall impression:** On a scale of 1-10, how would you rate your overall experience with Guidera?

2. **Top 3 features:** What are the three features you liked most?

3. **Top 3 pain points:** What are the three things that frustrated you the most or need the most improvement?

4. **Missing features:** Is there anything you expected from a travel app that Guidera doesn't have?

5. **Trust:** Would you trust Guidera to help you plan and manage a real international trip? Why or why not?

6. **Recommendation:** Would you recommend this app to a friend who travels? What would you tell them about it?

7. **Price sensitivity:** If this app had a premium tier at $9.99/month with advanced features (AI planning, flight tracking, departure advisor), would you consider paying for it? What features would make it worth paying for?

8. **Comparison:** How does Guidera compare to other travel apps you've used (Google Trips, TripIt, Hopper, Airbnb, etc.)?

9. **Bugs:** List any bugs, crashes, or broken features you encountered during testing.

10. **Final thoughts:** Any other feedback, suggestions, or thoughts you'd like to share?
