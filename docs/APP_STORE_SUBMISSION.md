## 10. Legal & Regulatory

### Intellectual Property
- **Trademark**: "Guidera" — ensure trademark filing is in progress or complete
- **App Icon**: Original design, no third-party copyrighted material
- **Content**: All AI-generated content includes appropriate disclaimers
- **Third-party content**: Travel data sourced from licensed APIs (Amadeus, Booking.com, GetYourGuide, Viator)

### Regulatory Compliance
| Regulation | Status |
|-----------|--------|
| **GDPR** (EU) | Compliant — Privacy Policy Section 3, 5.2 |
| **CCPA** (California) | Compliant — Privacy Policy Section 5.3 |
| **COPPA** (Children) | Compliant — no users under 18 |
| **PCI DSS** (Payments) | Not applicable — no direct payment processing (third-party processors) |
| **HIPAA** | Not applicable — no health data |
| **ADA/WCAG** (Accessibility) | Partial — accessible labels on navigation, further audit recommended |

### Licenses & Permits
- No special licenses required for a travel information/booking aggregation app
- Guidera acts as an **intermediary/aggregator**, not a licensed travel agency
- Bookings are completed via third-party providers (Amadeus, Booking.com, etc.)
- Flight compensation claims facilitated, not directly processed

### Content Rights
- All content displayed is either:
  - User-generated (governed by Terms of Service Section 5)
  - AI-generated (using licensed AI APIs — OpenAI, Google AI)
  - Sourced from licensed data providers (travel APIs)
  - Original (created by Guidera team)
- No copyrighted music, videos, or images used without license
- Creator content (videos) on destination pages is sourced/embedded with appropriate rights

### Apple App Store Review Guidelines — Key Areas
| Guideline | Compliance |
|-----------|-----------|
| **4.0 Design** | Native UI, follows HIG patterns |
| **4.2 Minimum Functionality** | Full-featured app, not a thin wrapper |
| **4.3 Spam** | Unique app, not a duplicate |
| **5.1.1 Data Collection** | Privacy policy in-app and at URL |
| **5.1.2 Data Use and Sharing** | Declared in privacy labels |
| **3.1.1 In-App Purchase** | Subscriptions via Apple IAP (required) |
| **3.1.2 Subscriptions** | Auto-renewable, clearly described |
| **2.5.4 Background Modes** | Location for safety alerts (justified) |
| **5.2.1 Intellectual Property** | Original content, licensed APIs |

## Appendix: Apple Review Notes Template

Use this when submitting for review:

```
App Review Notes:

Guidera is an AI-powered travel companion app. Here's what you need to know:

1. DEMO ACCOUNT: [email] / [password] — pre-loaded with a sample trip

2. LOCATION: Allow location access when prompted. Many features (safety alerts, 
   nearby destinations, navigation) require location.

3. BOOKINGS: When you tap "Book" on a flight/hotel/car, the app redirects to 
   third-party booking sites. No real transactions occur in the app.

4. AI FEATURES: The AI chat, AI Vision translator, and Smart Trip Planner 
   require internet connectivity. Generation may take 15-60 seconds.

5. CAMERA: Used for scanning boarding passes/tickets, AR navigation, and 
   AI Vision (menu/sign translation). Not for general photo capture.

6. SUBSCRIPTIONS: Free tier available with limited AI features. Premium tiers 
   unlock unlimited AI chat, Smart Plans, and AI Vision.

7. COMMUNITY: Users can create/join groups, post content, and create events. 
   All content is moderated per our Community Guidelines.

8. BACKGROUND LOCATION: Used only for safety geofencing — sends alerts when 
   users enter high-risk zones while traveling abroad.

Contact: support@guidera.one | +[phone number]
```
